# Welcome to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the future of robotics education. This textbook provides a comprehensive journey from foundational concepts to advanced implementations in **Physical AI** and **Humanoid Robotics**.

## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI represents intelligence that exists beyond screens and digital environments. It's AI that:

- **Operates in the real world** with physical constraints
- **Perceives through sensors** like cameras, LIDAR, and IMUs
- **Acts through actuators** to manipulate its environment
- **Learns from embodied experience** rather than just data

Unlike traditional AI confined to data centers, Physical AI must understand physics, navigate spaces, and interact safely with humans.
